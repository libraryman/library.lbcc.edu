New Library Website

2015 09 30

Long Beach City College Library is happy to announce its new website design! The new site has most of the same content as the old one, but has a responsive layout that works on smart phones and tablets. Please share your thoughts on our design by sending an email to Ward Smith.

Basic Navigation

Any time you want to see a list or a map of all the website’s contents, click on the menu icon with your mouse for other navigation options

Click on the Library logo to return to the home page.

Doing Research

Our new web page is structured more like an app. The information from the previous website is here, in much the same structure. On the top of the home page, you can see a row of buttons for starting your research:

You can find out how to contact us, what is happening at the library, and how to get involved by scrolling down. You will see major areas linked with red buttons the “Find” row, but also see news articles and announcements about happenings in the Library.Of course you can always click on the menu icon at the top left of the browser window to see a full site map and index.
