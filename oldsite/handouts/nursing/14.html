Other Database Resources

The Library also provides access to:

PubMed
Free service from the National Library of Medicine, providing access to citations from medical literature. No full text available. Use the library's free service to request articles. You must plan for delivery time. See complete instructions on library homepage.

CINAHL Direct
Medical and nursing information database providing access to citation and summary. No full text available. Article may be ordered via fax or mail through CINAHL or requested through the Library's Interlibrary Loan service.

Health Reference Academic
Full-text database providing articles from medical journals and other publications focused into the health fields. Password required.
