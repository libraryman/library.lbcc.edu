Here to Serve
All Your Information Needs:

The "Three Dimensional"
LBCC Library



LBCC students, staff, and faculty may not all realize it, but through the years their familiar LAC and PCC libraries have morphed into something different-- and better. Today you have three options when you look to the library to help identify, retrieve and deliver the information you're looking for:

  • Our Physical Library
    The first thing that may come to mind when the library is mentioned is probably is the L or GG building and its excellent collections of books, periodicals, microforms, videos, and cd's, carefully chosen to meet the needs of the LBCC curriculum

  • Our Extended Library
    Thanks to our partnership with CSU Long Beach Library your LBCC college id will let you check out books from that million-volume collection and use its facilities, which are open until midnight most nights, Sundays, and have even longer hours before final exam time. We'll also borrow books and journal articles for you from libraries near and far through our Interlibrary Loan service.

  • Our Virtual Library
    Though our buildings contain a wide range of print material, the scope and breadth of those collections is enormously enriched by the access the library provides its campus constituents to web-based information resources. For example, the library has subscriptions to 146 current print journals; but it offers full text access to more than 2000 additional titles through its subscription to the ProQuest research library, and they are available anytime, anyplace from any web-based computer. The virtual library contains over 8000 e-books, as well as reference materials, newspapers, government documents, and statistical information, all delivered to your desktop.
