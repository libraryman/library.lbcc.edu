Experience the

Power of Partnership

As the semester draws to a close and as finals approach it's a good time to remember that LBCC Libraries have established a partnership with the University Library at CSU Long Beach. What does partnership mean for LBCC students, staff, and faculty:

  • Use your College Service card at CSULB to check out items from a collection of 1.4 million volumes, plus VHS and DVD videos, CD's, maps, and federal and state documents


    •
  • Enjoy our partner library's longer operating schedule, including evenings until midnight, Sundays all afternoon and evening


    •
  • Take advantage of CSULB Library's special extended schedule for finals: open until 3 a.m. weeknights, plus Friday and Saturday nights.
