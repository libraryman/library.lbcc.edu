Send the Library a Message--

Get an Answer!

LBCC Library’s staff and librarians want to hear your ideas for improving the library’s collections and services.  Now, thanks to a new feature on the library homepage, it’s easier than ever to send us your questions, suggestions, and acquisitions recommendations, compliments and complaints.  Just point your web browser to http://lib.lbcc.edu/  and click on the picture of the suggestion box..  Just complete the form and click “submit”; we’ll send a response within 10 days to anyone who provides an e-mail address. 

“Our electronic suggestion box will help us communicate better with the students, staff, and faculty at LBCC,” said Dele Ukwu, Library Department Head; “we always try to listen to our constituents and to insure that our programs and information resources are meeting their needs.”
