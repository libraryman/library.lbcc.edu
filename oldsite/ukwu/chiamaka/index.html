Welcome to Dele's web site

Nno (Igbo (Ibo), Nigeria) Ekabo (Yoruba, Nigeria) Sannu (Hausa, Nigeria)


Chiamaka is an Igbo name. It means "God is great/wonderful/beautiful ..." Although Chiamaka is a personal name, I chose it as the name of my company after several considerations for many reasons. First, it combines the names of almost all members of my family. Second, it is unique, uncommon, and easy to pronounce. Third, it is quite simply a beautiful name.

I come from a rich and proud ethnic background. I value nature and treasure its beauty. I am greatly attracted to fabrics/textiles (as if this news will come as a surprise to all who know me). Our African fabrics have vibrant colors and are most often named after something or someone, signifying their importance as part of our lives.

I use African fabrics and prints in designing ornaments and clothes. My designs are very special to me and I take great pride and care in personally putting my ornaments together. They represent diversity and are a blend of multicultural sentiment. I have tried to convey the aesthetics of my cultural heritage and diversity through my ornaments and my other designs. I hope that my designs will bring as much joy to you as they have to me.

I will be honored to receive your comments. Please drop me a line. Thank you for visiting.

Dele Chinwe Ladejobi Ukwu
