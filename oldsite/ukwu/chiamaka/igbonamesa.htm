Abazu

male

You never get rich enough (so be satisfied with what you have).

Achebe

male

This is a short form of longer names with various meanings. "Chebe" means to protect or protect. Anichebem means may the land (or the land deity) protect me. Chichebem or Nnachebem means may God or god protect me.

Adaeke See also, Mgbeke

Adaugo

female

Ugo, the Eagle, is a symbol of purity, excellence and strength. Hence, Adaugo literally translates, the excellent or unparalleled daughter. Similar to Akwaugo. See also, Ugochukwu or Ugonna.
