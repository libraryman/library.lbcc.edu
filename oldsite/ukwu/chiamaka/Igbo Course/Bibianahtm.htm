Igbo People: Language & Culture

Welcome to the Igbo Language Classes

Hello!! My name is Mrs. Bibiana Tochukwu Onyeama. I was born in Enugu State, Nigeria. I am the coordinator of the Igbo classes.

Education

Teacher's Grade Two Cert. (T.C.II), Grade II Teacher Training Women Teacher Training College, Enugu, Nigeria.

Professional Certificate in Education (PCE), Professional Course in Education Institute of Ecumenical Education

Degree of Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), Administration and Supervision/Political Science University of Science and Technology, Enugu

Diploma, Local Economic Development Marshaw Training in Israel

Professional background

I have been a teacher and have taught in several elementary schools in Nigeria since 1977. I became an education officer in the State of Enugu in 1997.

I speak Igbo fluently and have taught the language in schools for several years. I also taught Igbo to Igbo children in the United States at the Igbo Camp held yearly at the California State University, Dominguez Hills (CSUDH).

I encourage you to suggest improvements in the structure and delivery of the classes as we go along. Please send your comments and/or suggestions to chiamaka.com

The Igbo Online classes are made possible through the generosity of Dele Chinwe Ukwu and is hosted by chiamaka.com, the site dedicated to promoting the languages and cultures of Nigerian tribes. As you are aware, all classes incure costs. We will appreciate any assisance in kind or in cash to the teaching of the Igbo or any other Nigerian language(s) and culture(s). Please let us know how you can help.

Thank you for your time.
