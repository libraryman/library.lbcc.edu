Ugochukwutubelum or Ugonnatubelum See also Adaugo

male

"Ugo," the Eagle, is a symbol of purity, excellence, and strength. Both "Nna" or "Chukwu" mean God. Hence, this name literally translates, God's annointed one;
A gift of purity from God.

Uru(nna)(nwa)(ahia)
See also Nwamaka

female

This is one of the names that can only be interpreted within the context of its usage. Urunna=benefits of a father; Urunwa = rewards of a child (it is rewarding to have a child); uruahia=market profit. A child is usually named Urunna or Urunwa.
Uru
