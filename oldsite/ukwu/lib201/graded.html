All assignments must be turned in by their due date. Please refer to the Calendar for each assignment's due date. To receive credit for the course, in addition to the bibliography, all eight assignments must be turned in by the end of the course. No exceptions.

When working on assignments, it's often handy to have a print copy. The assignment pages are styled for print. When you press Control-P (Windows) or Command-P (Mac OS), the page will be formatted for print. No special plug-ins needed.
