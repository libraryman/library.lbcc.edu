January 16, 2007

Welcome to Lib 201, Introduction to Cataloging! I'm happy you're taking this course.

To begin, please access Course Information by clicking on the button to your left and learn about the topics we'll be covering in this Web-based course. You will also learn how the course is organized.

After that, go ahead and explore all the other features available on this site by clicking on the other buttons to your left. Practice navigating this site—that is, steering your way through the course pages in a point-and-click process—to find specific course-related information. I especially want you to know how to access Long Beach City College Library's online databases and links to relevant Internet sites by clicking on the Bibliography button to the left. Later on, I'll teach you how to use these resources.

I'm interested in getting to know the students in this class, and perhaps you would like to learn a little something about your virtual classmates. So, please, go to the Student Biographies page, by clicking on the relevant button to the left, and enter two or three sentences about yourself. That way, we can become acquainted with one another.

By posting this information, please be aware that you authorize your instructor to publish this information on our class Web site and agree to receive e-mail from other students in this class.

One final note: Remember to check this page often, as this is where I will post news and updates to the course.

Dele
