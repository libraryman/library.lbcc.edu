Final Assignment: Bibliography

Instructions

Submit a bibliography (known as a "works cited" page in MLA) related to your topic. Please use MLA documentation style for all citations. I need two citations for each of the following categories:

  • Reference books = two citations
  • Circulating books = two citations
  • Newspaper articles = two citations
  • Popular magazine articles = two citations
  • Scholarly journal articles = two citations
  • Internet resources = two citations

Total = 12 citations

You may have already obtained some of the required citations from previous assignments. If so, all you need do is list them in the proper order, according to MLA style.

To obtain the citations, perform searches in the resources we have studied, such as Voyager, ProQuest, and the Internet.
