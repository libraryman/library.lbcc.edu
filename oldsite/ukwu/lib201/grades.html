Grades

Check your grades for this course.

Grading is based upon the completion of the assignments and bibliography and participation on the discussion forums. Each week, you will be asked to e-mail a completed assignment to your instructor; the bibliography is due on the final week of the course. You may earn up to 11 points for the eight graded assignments, and 12 points for the bibliography, for a total of 100 points. In order to earn a grade of "CR," you must accumulate a total of at least 70 points.

Here is the point breakdown for students who have opted for a letter grade:

  • A   90-100 points
  • B   80-89 points
  • C   70-79 points
  • D   60-69 points
  • F   below 60 points
