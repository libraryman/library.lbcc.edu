How to obtain a free e-mail account

 

At the orientation session, I will walk you through the procedure of obtaining a free e-mail account at one of the Web sites.

You may also sign-up for an e-mail account before the class starts. The following Web sites offer free e-mail accounts in exchange for advertising. They each have their own registration procedures. Please follow their step-by-step instructions on how to fill out the form. The following links will take you directly to the sign-on screen:

Hotmail

Netscape

Altavista

Yahoo

You will be asked to choose your own username and password. Write your selected username and password down and keep them for future reference.

Submit the form and you will instantly get an e-mail account.

Try to send a message to a friend or to an instructor. Look on the screen for the proper procedure to send a message.
