Instant Messaging Reference Service
@ Long Beach City College Library
 

Get instant help!

Have a question?
Use the meebo widget to the left to send an instant message to an LBCC librarian without downloading software. Just type your message in the lower rectangle, and press "Enter" or "Return." You may also sign up for a free account through meebo.

Get an alternative text version

Privacy Information
LBCC Library may save the transcript of your chat session for training and statistical purposes. Any identifying patron information is stripped out. The Library is committed to protecting your privacy; any personal information you provide will remain confidential.

LBCC Library does not endorse meebo.

You may also use your favorite instant messenging service to ask a librarian at Long Beach City College a question. Just add our screen name to your contact (or buddy) list to start. For help using IM, please visit our IM Reference FAQ page.
