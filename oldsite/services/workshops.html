Library Workshops
 

Attend a Workshop

  • Convenient drop-in hours during the school day - come on in at any time during the scheduled workshop hour.
  • Several topics to choose from to help you improve your research and information competency skills
  • Personalized instruction from a Librarian

LBCC Library Upcoming Workshops

Workshop Topics:

  • ARTstor - Learn how to search the digital collection of more than one million images in the arts, architecture, humanities, and social scienes. Offered most Thursdays @ LAC Campus from 2 pm to 3 pm: Librarian Celina Lee. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

  • Elements of MLA Citation - Learn how to create a bibliography in MLA style. Offered most Tuesdays @ LAC Campus from 2 pm to 3 pm: Librarian Karen Vogel. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

  • Health and Nursing Resources - Learn strategies for identifying and locating nursing research articles. Offered most Thursdays @ LAC Campus from 10 am - 11 am: Librarian Nenita Buenaventura. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

  • Investigación en Bases de Datos - El catalogo publico en linea recursos de referencia bases de datos en linea libros electrónicos recursos en la internet caminata en la biblioteca. Offered most Fridays @ LAC Campus from 10 am - 11 am: Librarian Ruben Amador. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

  • Public Library Academic Resources - Learn how to use free public library resources for school and beyond. Offered Most Mondays @ LAC Campus from 10 am - 11 am: Librarian Ted Kane. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

  • Research Paper Basics - This workshop will focus on the entire process of writing a basic research paper and also how to correctly cite sources using MLA and APA formats. Offered Wednesdays @ LAC Campus from 10 am to 11 am: Librarian Gabriel Beeler. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

  • Textbooks on Reserve at the Library - Learn to use the Library's Textbook Reserves and cut costs on the amount you spend on books. Offered Mondays @ LAC Campus from 2 pm - 3 pm: Librarian Karen Vogel. Check the calendar above for upcoming sessions.

For More Information

At the Liberal Arts Campus: Speak to a Librarian at the reference desk in the library located in Building L, or call the reference desk at (562) 938-4232.

At the Pacific Coast Campus: Visit the Librarian at the reference desk in the library or call the reference desk at (562) 938-3028.
