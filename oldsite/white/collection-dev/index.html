Collection Development Policy Statement

Purpose

The Long Beach City College Collection Development Policy is intended to guide librarians and instructors in the selection of materials to be added to the Library's collections, whether by purchase, through gifts, or by participation in consortia or partnerships—such as the Council of Chief Librarians, the California State University system, and the Community College League. This policy informs the College community and general public of the principles upon which selections are made.
