Types of Subject Directories

There are two types of subject directories:

  • commercial
  • academic

Commercial subject directories, such as Yahoo!, have no or very few annotations.



Academic subject directories, such as Librarians' Internet Index and Infomine, are reviewed by librarians and information specialists. Detailed annotations are given with each website listed.
