Are the World Wide Web (WWW) and the Internet the same thing?

Technically, no. The WWW is one part of the Internet. The WWW is a huge collection of text and multimedia documents and other files and network services on the Internet.

The WWW makes it much easier to navigate the Internet by using a graphical-user interface to enter search queries in order to find related information.
