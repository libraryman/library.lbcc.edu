Evaluating Information

Internet resources are subject to the same evaluation criteria as printed resources. Here are the basic questions to ask yourself:

  • Accuracy: Who wrote the page?
  • Authority: Who published the document?
  • Objectivity: What opinions (if any) are expressed by the author?
  • Currency: When was it updated?
  • Coverage: Is the information presented cited correctly?
