So, What Is XHTML?

XHTML is the next generation markup languge of the Web.


XHTML is the reformulation of HTML 4.01 as an XML application. It is a markup language that combines the best of HTML and XML.


In a nutshell, this means that HTML will now conform to the stricter syntax and structure of XML. By conforming to these rules, your Web pages will

  1. maintain backwards compatibility as HTML, and
  2. be ready to move into the future of XML, which is opening the door to the expansion and full potential of the Web.
