Learning Outcomes

The goal of this course is to introduce you to XHTML and the steps necessary to design and develop websites effectively. After completing this course, you will be able to

  • identify the major phases of website design and the activities involved within each phase
  • use XHTML to hand-code Web pages
  • create CSS to control the presentation of a Web page
  • identify accessibility issues and write code to make your pages accessible to a variety of user agents
  • list at least three usability issues and explain how they influence website design
  • publish your website using FTP
