When to use the OED?
nFind a term when you know the meaning but have forgotten the word;
nFind the history of a familiar phrase;
nFind words that have come into English via a particular language;
nSearch quotations from a specific year;
nSearch for all quotations from a particular author and/or work;
nGain unique online access to at least 1,000 new and revised words each quarter;
nSee how language changed and how new scholarship has increased understanding of our linguistic and cultural heritage.
