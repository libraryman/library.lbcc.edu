How to Search
n  “Find Word” search
n A quick search which finds main dictionary entries, such as “alphabet,” “break,” “xylophone.”  It also finds subordinate entries such as the phrase in alphabet, or compounds such as “alphabet book.”  The search form is located at the top right of the browser window.
n 
n“Full text” search
n A full search of the entire dictionary text.  It finds your term wherever it occurs in the dictionary.  This could be in the form of an entry name, part of another word’s definition, in a quotation, etc.
n
n The search form is displayed by clicking the button in the bottom left corner of the screen marked “Simple Search.”
