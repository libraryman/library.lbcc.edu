What are the coverage of the OED?
nThe OED includes:
n
nThe meaning, history, and pronunciation of over a half a million words;
nThe usage of words through 2.5 million quotations from a wide range of international English language sources, from classic literature and specialist periodicals to film scripts and cookery books;
nEtymological analysis, lists of variant spellings, and pronunciation using the International Phonetic Alphabet;
nWords from across the English-speaking world, from North America to South Africa, from Australia and New Zealand to the Caribbean.
