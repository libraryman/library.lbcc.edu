Citing the OED
nWhen citing the OED, the editors of the OED recommend the following:
n
nExample of reference to an entry from the Second Edition:            
n “plough, n2.”       Oxford English Dictionary. Ed. J. A. Simpson and E. S. C. Weiner.
n 2nd ed. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1989. OED Online. Oxford University Press. 31 Oct. 2003.  http://oed.com/cgi/entry/00181778
n
nExample of reference to an entry from the New (third) Edition:
n
n “magistrate, n.” Oxford English Dictionary. Ed. John Simpson, 3rd.ed. OED Online. Draft Mar. 2000. Oxford University Press. 31 Oct. 2003. http://oed.com/cgi/entry/00299451>
n
nSee the web site at http://www/oed.com/public/guide/citing.htm
nfor additional recommendations.
